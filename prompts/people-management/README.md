# People Management

The human side of engineering leadership. These prompts help with the conversations, documents, and decisions that shape your team members' careers and experiences.

## When to Use These

- **Review cycle:** Performance review draft, feedback synthesis, promotion case
- **Weekly:** One-on-one prep, skip-level meeting prep
- **Hiring:** Job descriptions, interview rubrics
- **Hard moments:** PIP documentation, difficult conversation prep
- **Team building:** Onboarding plans, career ladders, team health analysis

## ⚠️ A Note on Privacy

These prompts often involve sensitive information about your team members. When using AI to draft people-related documents, be mindful of your company's data policies. Consider using anonymised or abstracted examples when possible, and never store AI-generated drafts in shared spaces without review.
