**Interview Rubric: Senior Backend Engineer**

**Loop Overview**
| Stage | Duration | Interviewer | Assessing |
|-------|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Phone screen | 45m | Recruiter + EM | Motivation, basics |
| System design | 60m | Staff eng | Distributed systems |
| Coding | 60m | Senior eng | Problem solving |

---

**Stage 2: System Design Rubric**

*Prompt/Format:* Design a URL shortener at scale.

| Rating | Observable Behaviours |
|--------|----------------------|
| **Strong Hire** | Proposes idempotency and sharding unprompted |
| **Hire** | Sound design with light guidance |
| **No Hire** | Misses major bottlenecks |
| **Strong No Hire** | Cannot produce a coherent design |

---

**Debrief Guide**
- Each interviewer shares rating and top observations before discussion.
- Disagreement: focus on demonstrated behaviour, not opinions.
- Decision: Hire requires Hire+ from 3 of 4 stages with no Strong No Hire.
